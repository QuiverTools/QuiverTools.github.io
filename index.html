A toolset to work with quivers
and moduli of quiver representations

QuiverTools is available as a SageMath library and as a Julia package. To install the version of your choice, follow its instructions:

  • SageMath version
  • Julia version

An introduction to the mathematics behind QuiverTools can be found in arXiv:2506.19432.

If you use QuiverTools in your research, please cite it. The following BibTeX entry is also a good starting point: 

@software{quivertools,
  author = {Belmans, Pieter and Franzen, Hans and Petrella, Gianni},
  title = {QuiverTools},
  url = {https://quiver.tools},
  doi = {10.5281/zenodo.1268022},
}

QuiverTools is developed by Pieter Belmans, Hans Franzen and Gianni Petrella.
